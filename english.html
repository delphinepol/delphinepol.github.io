I defended my PhD in Mathematics at the University of Angers in December 2016 in France. I was then postdoc at Hokkaido University in Japan, and then at the Technical University of Kaiserslautern in Germany.

Research topics

During my PhD and my postdocs, I worked mainly on the following topics:
  • a particular type of singularities called "free singularities"
  • a particular case of free singularities: the curves
  • subspace arrangements
  • singularities constructed from graphs

Programming

From October 2020 to September 2021, I was developer for the computer algebra system OSCAR, written in Julia. My role was to:
  • develop a structure for algebraic curves
  • implement functions to work with algebraic curves
  • write the corresponding documentation
  • write tests for the continuous integration via GitHub actions
During my research work, I used the computer algebra software Singular. As part of my research I wrote the following Singular-library for free singularities in collaboration with Raul Epure:
  • logmodules.lib
